About the game


This game is being made in Solidity using Remix IDE.

It is currently in open alpha for the contract.

Instructions for testers:


1: Use newObject(0, sectorId) to make a new star at the given sector.


2. Use newObject(1,starId) to make a new planet at the given star.


3. Use newObject(2, sectorId) to make a new station at the given sector.


4. Use newShip(stationId, shipClass) to make a new ship at the given station.
Make sure to check the shipClasses stats and costs before choosing which.


5. Use produceStars and producePlanets to harvest from your stars/planets.
There is one cooldown for all a players stars, and another for all their planets.
The energy and matter is stored in the sector it's produced in, and ship's are required to transport it.
Trade was planned for this version, but contract size was too large. This is being worked on.


6. Use produceDrones(stationId, amount) to produce an amount of drones at that station.
It cost 5 x station level * amount per drone. Drones are use for defending objects.


7. Use defendObject(type, object id, amount) to send drones to defend one of your objects.
Type 0 is star, 1 is planet, 2 is station, and 3 is sector. You can send drones to any owned sector.


8. Use shipAttackObject(shipId, 3, sectorId) to attack a sector, you should try to take over the sector your star is in.


9. Use nameSector(id, name) to spend 1 finney to name your sector, if you managed to take it over.


10. Use shipBattleShip(shipId, enemyId) to battle an enemy ship in the same sector as you.


11. Use shipMoveSectors(shipId, numpad-direction) to move your ship in the given direction (8 north, 2 south, 9 north east, etc)
Use direction 5 to use the sector's wormhole, if it's been discovered yet.


12. Use shipExplore() to look for resources in the sector your ship is in. Maybe you can find the ship's wormhole.


13. Use shipRepair() if your ship's hull is damaged. For now, this is free.


14. Use shipStorageManage(shipId, dir, type, amount) to move energy/matter to and from a ship.
For dir, true means deposit, false means withdraw. For type, true means energy, false means matter.


15. Use objectLevelUp(type, id) to spend energy and matter to level up your objects to increase their production/stats.
For types, 0 is star, 1 planet, 2 station, 3 sector, 4 ship. Costs increase as levels increase.


16. Use objectUpgrade(type, id) to spend 1 finney to upgrade the output stat of objects, which cannot change any other way.
This stat is set on creation, and is random within a range. This function increases by a set amount each use.


17. Use the public getter functions on the contract's EtherScan page to access game info while awaiting the front-end


Thank you for your patience and please contact me with any feedback!
