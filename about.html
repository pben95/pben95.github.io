About the game


This game is being made in Solidity using Remix IDE.

It is currently in open alpha for the contract.

Instructions for testers:


Energy used for moving ship, matter used for ship combat, gems used for leveling and upgrading ship.

1. Use the EtherGalaxy button to join the game and get a free ship.

2. Use produce energy and matter to get resources, has a cooldown.

3. Input a numpad direction to use shipMove, which moves in that direction.

4. shipExplore explores the sector your ship is currently in. Has a cooldown

5. shipMine mines the area it's in. Chance to find gems.

6. Use shipSkirmish to fight another ship. Enter enemy ship ID in input 1.

7. Use shipAssault to assault a sector. Energy sector ID in input 1.

8. Use produceDrones to build drones from energy and matter at a station you own. Requires owning a station.

9. Use defendSector to send drones to defend a sector. Protects from invaders.

10. Use equipSector to increase a sectors return damage on attacking enemy ships.

11. Use levelUp with 1 to level up a station, 2 to level up your player, and 3 to level up a sector.

12. Use levelUpShip to upgrade a ship stat. Must be at a station that can upgrade that stat, and has a high enough level.

13. Buying and selling resources is done in amounts of 100 only.

Thank you for your patience and please contact me with any feedback!
