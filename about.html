About the game


This game is being made in Solidity using Remix IDE.

It is currently in open alpha for the contract.

Go to MyEtherWallet, change the network to Rinkeby, and change MetaMask to Rinkeby.

Go to the contracts page, use the contract address and ABI from the link in the upper right.

Instructions for testers:

1: Use freeStuff() to get free energy and 50 units of material 1.

2: Use newStar(ID#) to get a star in the sector ID#. Between 1 and 225.

3: Use newStation(starID#) to get a station orbiting that star.

4: Use newShip(stationID#,1,1,1,1) to get a new ship at that station, using material 1 for all 4 components.

5: Use assetProduce(true, starId#) to produce energy from your star, true being a star, false being a planet.

6: Use exploreShip(shipId#) to explore the sector with your ship. You can find the sector's blackhole or wormhole.

7: Continue to use assetProduce and exploreShip on their cooldown to get more energy to use newPlanet(starId#).

8: Use assetSetProduction(planetId#, 1) to allow your planet to produce material 1, which is the only option at level 1.

9: Use assetProduce(false, planetId#) to convert energy into material 1. You cannot check your material balance in this contract.

10: Use assetProduceDrones(stationId#, amount, 1) to produce drones using material 1. For material 1, you get 1 drone/input.

11: Use defendAsset(type, id) or attackAsset(type, id) to defend/attack Stars/Planets/Stations/Sectors (types 0, 1, 2, 3).

12: Use shipMove(shipId#, numpadDirection) to move your ship. It costs 1 energy from your ships fuel (max 10) to move one sector, make sure you don't run out. Use shipDock(shipId#, false, stationId#) and shipRefuel(shipId#) to dock and refuel.
